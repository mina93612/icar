自傳 Biography

Hsin-Han Chiang received the B.S. and Ph.D. degree in electrical and control engineering from the National Chiao-Tung University in Hsinchu, in 2001 and 2007, respectively. He was a postdoctoral researcher in electrical engineering at the National Taipei University of Technology in 2008 and 2009. From Aug. 2009 to Jan. 2017, he was an Assistant and Associate Professor with the Department of Electrical Engineering, Fu Jen Catholic University in New Taipei City.

From Feb. 2017 to Jan. 2022, he was an Associate Professor with the Department of Electrical Engineering, National Taiwan Normal University in Taipei. Since Feb. 2022, he is an Associate Professor with the Department of Vehicle Engineering, National Taipei University of Technology in Taipei. His research interests are in the area of intelligent systems and control, autonomous driving systems, vehicle dynamics and control, and autonomous mobile robots.
